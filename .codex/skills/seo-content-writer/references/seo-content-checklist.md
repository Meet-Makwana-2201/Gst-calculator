# SEO Content Checklist

## Quick Checklist
- Primary keyword is clear and matches the page intent.
- Title leads with the topic and gives a concrete promise.
- Meta description explains the outcome and invites the click without sounding spammy.
- Introduction answers the query quickly.
- H2s cover the main subtopics a searcher expects.
- Examples, formulas, steps, or comparisons are included where relevant.
- Internal links point to the best calculator, related guide, or category page.
- FAQ section covers real follow-up questions instead of repeating the article.
- Metadata, canonical URL, and schema match the final slug and topic.

## Blog Post Blueprint
1. Title
2. Meta description
3. Primary keyword and secondary phrases
4. Search intent summary
5. Introduction with direct answer
6. H2: definition or core concept
7. H2: formula, steps, or method
8. H2: examples or scenarios
9. H2: mistakes, tips, or comparison
10. H2: why use the calculator or tool
11. H2: FAQs
12. Conclusion or next-step CTA

## Title Guidance
- Prefer 50 to 65 characters when possible, but do not cut clarity to hit a number.
- Put the primary keyword near the front.
- Use specific benefit language such as `with Formula`, `Examples`, `Rate Chart`, or `Calculator`.
- Avoid empty superlatives like `Best`, `Ultimate`, or `Amazing` unless the page proves the claim.

## Meta Description Guidance
- Aim for roughly 140 to 160 characters when possible.
- Mention the target topic, the practical outcome, and one differentiator.
- Good pattern: `Learn X, see Y, and use Z to get the result quickly.`

## FAQ Guidance
- Write 4 to 6 FAQs for most posts when there are genuine follow-up intents.
- Each question should target a distinct query variation.
- Answers should be concise, factual, and not depend on hidden context.

## Internal Linking Guidance
- Link early to the most relevant calculator or parent page if it helps the user.
- Add supporting links only where the surrounding paragraph makes the click feel natural.
- Prefer high-intent anchors like `GST Calculator`, `EMI Calculator`, or `GST rate chart` over generic `click here`.

## Repository Notes
- Blog posts in this repo are TSX components under `src/content/blog/posts`.
- Similar posts often export `metadata`, `toc`, `BlogPosting` JSON-LD, and `FAQPage` JSON-LD.
- Keep slugs, canonical URLs, image paths, and wrapper props in sync.
